NURBS_BEM_EMSolver

A mesh-free NURBS-based boundary element solver for solenoid magnetic-field computation. The project generates multifidelity electromagnetic datasets for surrogate training and motivates POD feasibility and parametric Operator Inference studies.

Surrogate-model-learning

A systematic study of surrogate methods from first principles, including Gaussian process regression, response surface methods, radial basis functions, uncertainty behavior, and failure modes of approximation.

Research direction

I am working toward a deeper mathematical understanding of why surrogate models generalize, how their uncertainty can be quantified, and where their assumptions break down.

Why it matters

These projects strengthen the technical side of my profile for digital twins, scientific ML, and decision tools where predictions need to be interpretable and uncertainty-aware.
