AE8900 · time-series ML · model interpretation

Finding structure in thrust-time profiles.

This project explores machine-learning methods for identifying failure cases in solid rocket motor thrust-profile data, with emphasis on feature extraction, clustering, validation, and careful interpretation.

Methods

  • Time-series feature extraction
  • Clustering and pattern discovery
  • Smoothing and preprocessing
  • Model validation and interpretation

Research question

Can thrust-profile time series reveal patterns that separate nominal behavior from potential failure cases, especially when labels are incomplete or uncertain?

What I learned

The project strengthened my interest in data-driven research, especially the need to connect ML outputs with domain knowledge, assumptions, and validation rather than treating models as black boxes.

Connection to broader work

Although the application is aerospace, the methods transfer to other systems problems: time-series analysis, uncertainty, feature extraction, and model interpretation are all useful for mobility, resilience, and digital-twin research.
